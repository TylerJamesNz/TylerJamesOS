Pop-out dock

Floating actions (e.g. theme helper) open a dock: a narrow sheet slides out beside a wider anchor panel. The pair shares one visual seam — rounded outer corners only, no double border between them. Use this for secondary steps without a full modal. Short feedback belongs inside the sheet as a compact notice (role="status", aria-live="polite"), not a browser alert. The live sheet mirrors theme-helper.css classes; dark mode in the app applies only to the Custom theme.
