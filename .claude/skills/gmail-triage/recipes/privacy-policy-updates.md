# Privacy policy / terms updates

Noise: ToS updates, privacy statement changes, policy notifications. No action required.

## Match

Subject or body contains: `updates to our terms`, `privacy policy`, `privacy statement update`, `we're updating our policy`, `changes to our terms of service`.

## Confidence

Always high — narrow pattern.

## Action spec

1. **Archive** on approval.
2. No sender-list update — these come from anywhere.

## Approval prompt

Batched with other obvious archives. No individual prompt needed beyond the default archive list.
